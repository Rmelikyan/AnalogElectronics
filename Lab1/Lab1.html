Solving for $R_{12}$ and $R_{23}$ as a function of $V_{out}$¶

$R_{12} + R_{23} = R_{13}$¶
$V_{out} = V_{In} \cdot R_{23}/(R_{12} + R_{23})$¶
$V_{out} = V_{In} \cdot R_{23}/(R_{13} - R_{23} + R_{23})$¶
$V_{out} = V_{In} \cdot R_{23}/R_{13}$¶

$ R_{23} = V_{out} \cdot R_{13} / V_{In}$¶
